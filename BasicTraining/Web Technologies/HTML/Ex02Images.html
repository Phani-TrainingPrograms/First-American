Mysore Dist

Mysore was the Capital of the Wodeyar Dynasty that lasted for 400 Years from 16th Century to 20th Century until it was combined into the Republic of India. The last King of the Wodeyar Dynasty was Sri Jayachamaraja Wodeyar. He continued as MP for the City of Mysore till his death.

Vijayanagar Dist

The Empire of Vijaynagar was from the 13th Century to 16th Century with its prime time in the late 15th and early 16th Century. It's ruins are one of the famous UNESCO Sites in India. It is now called as "HAMPI"

Kolar Dist

Kolar is a town near Bangalore(65 Kms) that has lot of tourist attractions and good amount of treking places. The Antargange Hills gives a nice Landscape of Green hills to trek. The Avani Hills is also a nice place to trek for 500 Steps and have glimpse of the green Fields and Water bodies.
